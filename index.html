How it works

Starting from a proven agent library, we adapt each solution to your exact workflows - your ERP, CRM, internal tools, or custom systems. If it doesn't exist yet, we build it.

Customer insights

What leaders are actually seeing.

Patterns and findings from GoAI deployments across industries and company sizes.

Insight - Strategy
78% of executives say agentic AI requires reinventing their operating model

Solo agents are giving way to coordinated multi-agent systems. Organizations that treat agentic AI as a drop-in tool rather than a structural shift are seeing limited ROI - those rebuilding workflows around it are seeing transformational results.

April 2026 - UiPath Agentic Automation Trends Report
Insight - Operations
Business users are now the second-fastest growing automation group

In 2025, HR, finance, and sales teams became active builders of automation workflows - not just consumers. 63% of organizations now report over 200 self-service automation users, signaling automation has left the IT department for good.

March 2026 - Stonebranch Global State of IT Automation
Insight - Technology
The IPA market hit $18B in 2025 - and is still accelerating

Intelligent Process Automation expanded from $16B in 2024 to $18B in 2025 at a 12.9% CAGR. The shift driving it is decision-making capability - automation is no longer just task execution, it's contextual judgment at scale.

February 2026 - Industry Market Analysis
Insight - Leadership
Adoption is a design problem, not a training problem

Organizations that struggle with AI adoption almost always have an integration gap, not a skills gap. When agents connect to the tools teams already live in, usage is immediate and sustained - without any formal training required.

January 2026 - GoAI Deployment Analysis
Insight - Governance
Governance-as-code is becoming the new baseline for enterprise AI

As AI agents take on more autonomous decision-making, organizations in regulated industries are requiring platforms to show how decisions are made, how models are trained, and how data flows. Transparency is no longer optional - it is the purchase criteria.

December 2025 - Enterprise AI Governance Review
Insight - Infrastructure
77% of enterprises now run hybrid environments - and cloud-only is declining

The cloud-or-on-premise debate is settled: most enterprises need both. Organizations are shifting focus from migration to optimization - fine-tuning how workloads are distributed across environments to balance performance, cost, and compliance.
